How to Join NHC SMP

1

Join Our Discord

Start by joining our Discord server to connect with the community and begin your application.

Join Discord Server
2

Submit Your Application

Fill out our application form in Discord to tell us about yourself and your Minecraft experience.

See application details below
3

Application Review

Our staff team will review your application. This usually takes 24-48 hours during active periods.

Typical wait time: 2-5 days
4

Welcome to NHC!

Once approved, you'll be whitelisted and can join the server to start your adventure!

Application Questions

When you apply in Discord, you'll be asked these questions. Feel free to prepare your answers beforehand!

Basic Information

Name (optional) What would you like us to call you?
Pronouns Your preferred pronouns
Minecraft IGN Your Minecraft Java username
Age How old are you?
From (country/region) Where are you located?

Communication & Gaming

Voice Chat Preference Are you happy with talking on VC / proximity chat?
Minecraft Experience How long have you played Minecraft?
Activity Level How many days per week do you plan on playing?
Play Style How would you describe your play style?

Server Experience

Previous SMP Experience Have you played on community SMP servers before?
Build Portfolio Do you have any screenshots of previous builds you've completed?
Server Goals What are you looking to do on the server?
Your Plans What are your plans?
Build Ideas What builds do you want to do?
How You Found Us Where did you hear about NotHermitCraft? (mention any friends on the server)

Application Tips

  • Be honest and genuine in your responses
  • Tell us about your building style and interests
  • Mention if you know any current members
  • Screenshots of builds help us understand your experience level
  • Don't worry if you're new to SMPs - everyone starts somewhere!
